Premium Features

Donation Tracker

Effortlessly manage and track community donations with our transparent donation tracking system. Send reminders, generate reports, and maintain financial records with precision.

Gotra Directory

Maintain a comprehensive directory of community members organized by Gotra. Easily search, filter, and access member information with privacy controls.

Task Assignment

Assign, track, and manage community tasks efficiently. Set deadlines, monitor progress, and ensure accountability with our intuitive task management system.

Budget Tracking

Monitor community finances with precision. Track income, expenses, and generate detailed financial reports to ensure transparency and accountability.

Event Management

Organize and manage community events seamlessly. Schedule events, send invitations, track RSVPs, and coordinate all aspects of event planning in one place.

Notifications to Donor

Keep donors informed with automated notifications. Send donation receipts, event updates, and impact reports to build stronger relationships with contributors.

Media Archive

Store and organize community photos, videos, and documents in a secure digital archive. Easily share memories and important documents with members.

Language Support

Break language barriers with multi-language support. Communicate effectively with all community members in their preferred language.
